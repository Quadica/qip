# OM to QPM Transition Plan

**Document Creation Date:** Nov 12, 2025
**Last Update:** Nov 24, 2025
**Purpose:** Planning document to transition away from the original OM to the new Quadica Production Management system

---
## Our Current Product Lines

1. The current production system is geared towards building predefined LED modules
2. We currently have 700+ predefined LED modules on the luxeonstar.com site that customers can browse, review and add to the cart to order
3. Each module is fully detailed in WooCommerce and in the OM system so the module can be be built for stock or built when it is ordered
4. There are 4 different series of LED modules
   - SABER - This is the original module that we started the company with. A basic aluminum MCPCB high power LED module
   - CoolBase - A high power LED module built using FR4 technology. Also one of our original lines of modules
   - SinkPAD - This uses a MCPCB base technology with proprietary thermal cooling manufactured by Adura
   - SABER-2 - Our most recent series of aluminum MCPCB base LED modules, but using the latest non-proprietary MCPCB thermal technology
4. All of our LED modules are built using Lumileds Rebel LEDs only. The Rebel line of LED is nearing its end of life and needs to be replaced with an updated LED
5. We currently maintain a part binning system to hold and manage inventory
6. We also offer a design/built service for custom LED modules.
   - Over the years we have designed and produced about 50 different custom designs for specific customers
   - Custom modules are assigned a unique sku for inventory and production management
   - Custom modules are not listed on our website. They have been built for a specific customer
7. We also offer a number of supporting products, including:
   - LED optics, reflectors & holders from different manufacturers including
     - Carclo
     - Fraen
     - Polymer Optics
     - Khatod
     - Dialite
     - Ledil
   - A variety of low voltage LED drivers from LEDDynamics
   - A large selection of Heatsinks from Alpha
   - Thermal mounting tape that we manufacture to mount our LED modules to heatsinks
   - Arctic Silver thermal adhesive used to fasten LED modules to heatsinks
